Usefully Asked Questions

Last updated September 21, 2020.

These are some useful questions that people have asked me. Some of these have been asked very frequently, and others are questions that I believe people would benefit from knowing the answer to (even if they don't ask the question themselves).

  • What's your background? Who are you?

I'm Dennis Chen, sophomore, math enthusiast, and track/cross-country runner. (Unfortunate circumstances have left me unable to run cross country this fall.) Two-time AIME qualifier and USAJMO qualifier.

I liked the way your geometry book looks. How can I make something that looks similar?

  • I'm using a package called dennis.sty (developed for my own personal use). I have a video on how to import it. I hash out some specific dennis.sty tips and general guidelines in this thread, though using the style file correctly is bound to be difficult, so I suggest you email me if you need further assistance.

    While you're here, might I recommend shooting.cls?
  • I liked the preview for your geometry book. Where can I get the full version?

Nowhere, since as of right now it is not finished yet. If you take my classes you will be able to see more snippets of it.

  • Can you give me solutions to your mock AIME?

Sure, just PM me on AoPS or email me. Unless if you want a solution to #15, in which case, see below.

  • Can you give me a solution to #15 on your mock AIME?

Sorry, I seem to have misplaced the solution and forgot how to do the problem. I can tell you that the numerical answer is 19/30, but that's all that's in my notes.

  • Is the AMC class you ran going to rerun?

Yes. Expect future reruns as well.

  • Can I apply for MAST if I didn't make AIME/scored badly/etc?

Sure. I'll accept you if you demonstrate through the Diagnostic that you've gotten better between the competition and now and that you're ready for the program.

  • How did you make this website?

I wrote the HTML/CSS on this website myself. I learned some basic HTML/CSS on my own, using tutorials when necessary. I host on Github. The domain provider you use doesn't matter, but for the record, I'm using Namecheap. See here for my Github repository.

There are better people to ask than me though. If you want a website that actually does something, I recommend React. To put it mildly, I am no expert, so please ask someone else if you want to use React.

  • Why have some of your essays disappeared?

The pages are still up (so as to not screw over anyone who's trying to link to it), for the record. I remove essays if I later determine that a) they don't say anything that's already been said or b) the writing is exceptionally slipshod. I don't think this will happen often.

The one exception is "Mass Appeal," and it exists only to connect to Math Club, so that's here to stay.

  • Can I talk to you/hangout/ask you a question?

Feel free to slide into the DMs. I promise it won't kill you - after all, plenty of people have survived.
